Critical Issues

Top Pain Points

1

Three-Way Match Chaos

Descriptions rarely match across invoice, PO, and receiving report. Consumes ~50% of AP specialist time.

2

Shadow Excel Tracking

ERP lacks status visibility, forcing a personal spreadsheet for queue management — single-point-of-failure risk.

3

Duplicate Payments Slip Through

System can't flag duplicates with different invoice numbers or dates. 1-2 duplicates monthly.

4

Communication Bottlenecks

Waiting days to weeks for buyer/warehouse responses on discrepancies. Causes aging and late fees.

5

Broken Payment Notifications

Only 50% of vendors receive remittance advice, generating high volumes of "where's my payment?" calls.
